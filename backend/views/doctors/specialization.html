Doctor Specializations

Cardiologist

Heart specialist focused on diagnosing and treating heart-related conditions.

Dermatologist

Expert in skin, hair, and nail disorders, providing specialized skin care.

Pediatrician

Medical professional specializing in the care of infants, children, and adolescents.

Neurologist

Specialist in nervous system disorders, including the brain and spinal cord.

Orthopedist

Expert in diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal system disorders.

Nephrologist

Focuses on kidney health and treats kidney-related conditions and diseases.

Endocrinologist

Specializes in hormone imbalances and diseases affecting the endocrine system.

Psychiatrist

Medical doctor specializing in mental health, including substance use disorders.

Oncologist

Focuses on diagnosing and treating cancer and providing comprehensive care.

Pulmonologist

Specialist in respiratory system disorders, including the lungs and bronchial tubes.

Rheumatologist

Deals with rheumatic diseases and conditions involving joints, muscles, and ligaments.

Urologist

Specializes in urinary tract diseases and male reproductive organs.

Gynecologist

Focuses on women's reproductive health, including pregnancy and childbirth.

ENT Specialist

Treats conditions related to the ear, nose, and throat, as well as related structures.
